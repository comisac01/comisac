ComSIA 2026

3rd International Conference on Computing Systems & Intelligent Applications (ComSIA-2026) was held on 20th-21st March 2026 at School of Open Learning, University of Delhi, INDIA in association with Shaheed Rajguru College of Applied Sciences for Women, University of Delhi; Silicon University, India.

This conference was able to attract a diverse range of engineering practitioners, academicians, scholars and industry delegates, with the reception of 1900 papers from different parts of the world. Only 280 papers have been accepted and registered with an acceptance ratio of 15% to be published in the six volumes of the prestigious Springer Lecture Notes on Networks & Systems (LNNS) series.
