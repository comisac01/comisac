ComSIA 2024

First International Conference on Computing and Communication Systems for Industrial Applications (ComSIA 2024) was held on 10th -11th May 2024 at School of Open Learning, University of Delhi in collaboration with Shaheed Rajguru College of Business Studies, Delhi, India.

This conference was able to attract a diverse range of engineering practitioners, academicians, scholars and industry delegates, with the reception of more than 450 papers from different parts of the world.

Only 58 papers have been accepted and registered with an acceptance ratio of 13% to be published in one volume of CEUR Workshop Proceedings and one volume of the prestigious Springer Studies in Autonomic, Data-driven and Industrial Computing series.
