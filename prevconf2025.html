Previous Conference (ComSIA 2025)

Second International Conference on Computing Systems & Intelligent Applications (ComSIA 2025) was held on 28th -29th March 2025 at School of Open Learning, University of Delhi in collaboration with Shaheed Rajguru College of Business Studies, Delhi, India. This conference was able to attract a diverse range of engineering practitioners, academicians, scholars and industry delegates, with the reception of 960 papers from different parts of the world. Only 115 papers have been accepted and registered with an acceptance ratio of 12% to be published in the two volume of prestigious Springer Lecture Notes on Networks & Systems (LNNS) series.
