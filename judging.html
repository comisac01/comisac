Judging Criteria

Solutions will be evaluated across key dimensions to identify innovative ideas with a strong focus on AI implementation and ethical considerations.

  • 01 – Technical Innovation & AI Excellence (25%)
  • 02 – Security & Compliance Impact (25%)
  • 03 – Legal & Ethical Considerations (20%)
  • 04 – Scalability & Market Viability (15%)
  • 05 – Technical Presentation (10%)
  • 06 – Industry Impact & Innovation (5%)

Evaluation Process

Each submission will be scored across all criteria by a panel of expert judges. The weighted scores will determine category-wise winners as well as the overall winners.
