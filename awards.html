ComSIA Excellence in Research & Technology Awards

The Organizing Committee of ComSIA 2026 proudly announces a series of prestigious Awards and Recognitions to honor researchers, academicians, and professionals who have demonstrated outstanding contributions in computational research, data analytics, and industrial applications.

The awards will be conferred during the Valedictory Ceremony.

Selection Process Overview

A transparent, rigorous, and multi-stage evaluation framework

Call for Nominations

Nominations are invited from individual professionals, research teams, higher education institutions, innovation centers, start-ups, corporate units, and incubators.

Entries may be submitted through self-nomination, peer or professional recommendation, institutional leadership, or Advisory Council recommendations. All nominations must be supported by verifiable documentation and evidence of contribution.

Preliminary Review & Eligibility Check

Each nomination undergoes an initial screening to verify eligibility, completeness, and relevance to the selected award category.

Submissions lacking required documentation, failing eligibility norms, or falling outside the award scope will not proceed further.

Expert Scrutiny & Evaluation

Eligible nominations are evaluated by an eminent jury of academicians, senior researchers, industry leaders, policy advisors, and global domain experts.

Selection Criteria Framework

Criterion Focus Area
Original Contribution & Thought Leadership Novel ideas, intellectual depth, advancement of knowledge
Measurable Impact & Value Creation Demonstrated outcomes and real-world relevance
Technical Depth & Professional Excellence Rigor, quality, and domain expertise
Implementation Effectiveness Practical execution, efficiency, reliability
Leadership & Collaborative Approach Team building, partnerships, mentorship
Sustainability & Long-Term Relevance Scalability, continuity, future readiness
Ethical Conduct & Social Responsibility Responsible innovation and societal sensitivity

Each criterion is evaluated independently to ensure a balanced assessment of academic merit, industry relevance, and societal value.

Consolidation & Final Recommendation

Based on cumulative evaluations, a shortlist of high-ranking nominations is prepared. Scoring trends, expert observations, and supporting evidence are carefully reviewed during final consolidation.

Final Approval & Ratification

Final selections are ratified by the Awards Steering Committee comprising senior academicians, industry veterans, and independent observers.

Only nominations demonstrating exceptional merit, credibility, and sustained impact are approved. The committee reserves the right to withhold or reclassify awards if standards are unmet.

Transparency & Integrity Assurance

The selection process follows strict principles of fairness, confidentiality, and conflict-of-interest management. Jury decisions are final and binding to preserve the prestige and integrity of the awards.

Open Nominations and New Award Proposals

To encourage inclusivity and innovation, nominators may also propose new award categories not listed above. Such nominations should be justified with:

  • A clear description of the award theme or focus area
  • Rationale for its relevance to ComSIA 2026 themes
  • Evidence of achievement or contribution by the nominee

All proposed awards will be reviewed and approved by the Awards Committee before formal recognition.

Selection and Evaluation Process

  1. Call for Nominations: Peer, advisory, or self-nomination.
  2. Initial Screening (2–3 Days): Eligibility review.
  3. Expert Committee Evaluation (10–12 Days):
    • Innovation and Originality
    • Measurable Impact
    • Leadership and Collaboration
    • Global Contribution
  4. Final Review & Approval: Governance Council.

Selection Ratio: Top 5–7%

Evaluation committee details are available on the ComSIA 2026 website.
